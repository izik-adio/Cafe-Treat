About Us

Welcome to Cafe Treat, a realm where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee and the warmth of a cozy atmosphere greets you. Cafe Treat is more than just a coffee shop; it's an experience. Founded in 2022 by passionate coffee enthusiast Izik Adio, our cafe is a haven for those seeking the perfect cup, delectable treats, and a warm, inviting atmosphere.
